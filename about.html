My Roots

I grew up surrounded by rhythm — not just of drums and gongs, but of stories passed down in whispers, food served with intention, and hands moving to weave fabric and connection.

My roots lie in Indonesia, in traditions that taught me how to listen, move, and build. That sense of presence lives in every stage I dance on, every dashboard I craft, and every piece of digital space I shape.

Performing Arts

I was raised on movement. Saman, Tari Piring, the subtle gestures of storytelling through the body. These performances are more than cultural expressions to me. They are ways of remembering, ways of belonging.

Cultural Exchange

Across time in Ireland, Washington D.C., and beyond, I carry my heritage with me. It isn’t a relic. It’s a way of seeing — of building bridges between tradition and tomorrow with grace and intention.

“To move forward, I root deeper. And when I create, I return home.”
