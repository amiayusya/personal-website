A Soft Beginning

I was born in a place where stories are not just told — they are sung, danced, woven, and worn. In the folds of batik and the rhythm of traditional drums, I learned that memory doesn’t always live in words. Sometimes it lives in movement. In silence. In the pause before a performance begins.

As I grew, I carried those stories with me — across oceans, through classrooms, into code, dashboards, and photo frames. What you see here is not a resume. It’s a resting place for fragments. A space to gather what I’ve seen, what I’ve felt, and what I hope to pass on.

— Ayusya
